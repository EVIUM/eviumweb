• What is Evium?
  • EVIUM is the next generation of MMORPG (Massively Multiplayer Online Role Playing Game) created in Unreal Engine 5 and deployed to Web 3 using Ethereum and Polygon. Explore the open world, create clans, mine, build, earn, and complete quests and assignments with other players.
  • Evium Passport NFTs
  • Evium Passport NFTs are used to enter the Evium MMORPG. Passports unlock playable characters and your whole collection makes up your clan. Players can not enter the game without at least one passport nft. There are 4 kinds of passport including: 1st Edition, Shadowless, Base Set, and Japanese.
  • Evium MMORPG
  • The EVIUM MMORPG is situated on a 3-dimensional spherical globe game map. There are 7 geographical realms that are: Alpha, Beta, Gamma, Delta, Phi, Psi, and Omega. Each realm has unique terrain eccentricities, physic mechanics, and color palettes specific to its energy class. Evium of particular energy groups will excel in their relative realms.
  • Character Upgradability and Customization
  • Evium characters can earn upgrades from completing quests and are fully customizable. Don't like how your character looks? Simply adjust the appearance parameter settings in game. Parameters include: height, weight, face features, and apparel changes. Apparel can be found where it spawns on the map.
  • Play to Earn
  • Play for stakes with other players or rent out your Evium passports for cryptocurrency payments including: Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDC, Dogecoin, and others.
  • Game Modes
  • • Free-roam (Quests)
  • • Battle Royale
  • • Strategy (Civilization)
  • 3 game modes will be included in the game beta launch. Additional game modes TBA.
  • Evium Land NFTs
  • Land NFTs are plots of land on the MMORPG open world map. Each nft has unique quantities of natural resources including natural minerals, neural networks, and living organisms. Earn royalties from all monetary transactions on your land or created from resources mined from your land.
  • Evium Resources NFTs [Free Mint]
  • Evium Resources are natural resources that are required when completing certain quests and building objects in the MMORPG.
  • Kudos
  • Kudos coins are in-game respect points in the EVIUM MMORPG. Earn extra Kudos when teaming with others. Tip others with Kudos Coins when they assist you.

  • Evium Legend:
  • A short time after the birth of the universe, a large rogue planet originating from deep interstellar space called "EVIUM" was pulled into the orbit of a large gas giant. The powerful and dynamic gas giant was orbiting a binary star system in one of the first galaxies to form. EVIUM, rich in minerals and large quantities of inorganic matter, attained a non-toxic biosphere with liquid water and the EVIUM genesis flower called "Soltheos" emerged from the soil. Soltheos developed sentience and closely followed the skies of its new home in the universe, and gazed at the neighbouring constellations, absorbing all the information and storing it in genetic code. Over billions of years of stargazing, the flower had finally taken in enough solar energy, and had absorbed mystical and dark energies from the gas giant above. Soltheos started generating EVIUM lifeforms that would traverse through EVIUM as hunter gatherers. Soltheos gave rise to 7 Energy types which became: Alpha, Beta, Gamma, Delta, Phi, Psi, and Omega, and assigned Delta as its own energy group. 154 energy seeds spawned from the flower, from which 7 energy orbs and 147 EVIUM were born. Today, EVIUM lifeforms are exploring undiscovered frontiers and multiplying into clans and communites.
